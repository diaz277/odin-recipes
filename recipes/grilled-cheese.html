Grilled Cheese

Description

This crispy grilled cheese will only take 10 minutes to make and is perfect for any occasion. Whether you are looking for a quick snack or meal make sure to check this out!

  • 2 slices crusty Italian bread
  • 2 (1 ounce) slices white Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. SPREAD one slice of bread with Hellmann's速 or Best Foods速 Real Mayonnaise. Place bread Mayonnaise-side-down into hot skillet; add cheese.
